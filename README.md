## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a scene from the perspective of the 'grey shadow' slave in Chapter 1, observing the morning ritual of power.
- Create a private letter from Senator Rufus to his daughter, explaining his vote on the Moesian legion, revealing the pressure he feels.
- Draft the report from the urban prefect about the 'convenient' Subura fire, detailing what was found and what was quietly omitted.
- Compose the internal monologue of Tiberius Drusus Caesar during the Senate session, revealing what lies behind his 'winter sky' eyes.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive 'Hydraulics of Influence' network map, visualizing the characters as nodes of desire (wealth, security, legacy) and the transactions of power between them.
- Create an AI model that generates 'Senatorial gossip'—short, detached sentences floating in the air of the *palatium*—based on seed themes of scandal, ambition, and fear.
